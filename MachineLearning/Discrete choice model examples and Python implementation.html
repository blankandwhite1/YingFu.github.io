1. Introduction

1.1 Intuitive examples

Choice modeling models people's choices and decisions, whether they make decisions about consumer purchase, transport, activities or lifestyle.

Example1: A cosmetics company wants to promote a new product. The new product can be packaged in different forms, such as glass, plastic bottle and etc.; can be descripted with different words; can have different volumes; can be decorated with different designs and background colors; have different prices, and etc. The company wants to know the impact of each attribute on the customer's decision-making. Discrete choice model can be used in this way to quantify the impact of each attribute on the customer's decisions.

一个化妆品公司要推一个新产品，新产品可以有不同形式进行包装，如：玻璃瓶装、塑料管装等；也可以用不同的文字进行产品描述；也可以有不同的分量，如大、中、小等；使用不同的标签设计、背景颜色；价格等。公司需要知道每个因素对客户做决策的影响，可以通过离散选择模型来量化分析每一个因素对客户做选择的影响。

 

 

Example2: When we commute to work, we will face several modes of transportation means, such as walk, bike, bus, subway, cars and etc. For each mode, a herd of attributes are considered, such as in-vehicle travel time, out-of vehicle travel time, travel cost, transit counts and etc. For each mode, we will make "trade-offs" among these attributes and select one mode based on some rules, for example, maximum utility to make us satisfied. Bus operator can further forecast the effects of some new operating polices, such as increasing buses to reduce the out-of-vehicles times.

当我们通勤去上班时，我们会有很多种交通的方式：如步行、自行车、公交、地铁、自驾或滴滴等。对每个选择方式，我们都有一些属性衡量，比如：乘车时间、行走（或等待）时间、金钱成本、换乘次数等。对每种交通方式，我们都在它们的属性间作权衡取舍，并基于某些规则，如：效用最大化，去选择我们最满意的一种交通方式。一些公共交通的运营商可以分析一些运营方案（如增加公交数量来减少乘客的步行（等待)时间等）对公司营收的影响。

 

1. 2 Choice process

An decision maker(individual) first determines the available alternatives; next, evaluates the attributes of each alternative; then uses a decision rule to select an alternative. There are four elements in a choice process:

选择过程：一个决策者（消费者）首先确定他所有可行的选项有哪些，接着对每个选项的一些属性进行衡量，并基于一些决策规则去选择一个选项。选择过程有四个元素：

1). Decision maker (individual)

Different decision makers face different choice situations and can have different tastes. For example, two individuals with different incoming levels and different residential locations are likely to have different sets of modes to choose from and may place different importance weights on travel time, travel cost and other attributes.

不同的决策者有不同的决策处境，他们可能有不同选择偏好。比如：收入不同的决策者对每个选择属性的敏感度以及权重都不一样，相对高收入的人可能希望通勤时间更少，出行更舒适；而相对低收入的人群可能对交通成本更敏感。

2). Alternatives

Individuals will make a choice from a set of alternatives available to them. Set of available alternatives may be constrained by the infrastructure/supply. For example, public transportation, such as subway may not available for individuals who lives in the suburbs.

通勤方式有：步行、自行车、公交、地铁、自驾或滴滴等

通常这些选择会受到设施及供给的限制，比如：如果你居住在市中心，你可以有很多的出行方式选择，而如果你居住在郊区，可能就没有地铁出行。

3). Attributes of alternatives

The alternatives in a choice process are characterized by a set of attribute values. Attractiveness of an alternative is determined by the value of its attributes. The attributes of alternatives may be generic (that is, they apply to all alternatives equally) or alternative-specific (they apply to one or a subset of alternatives). For example, in-vehicle-time is usually a generic attribute for all motorized alternatives while out-of-vehicle-time in relevant to transit modes only.

有些属性是通用的，所有选项都有；但是有些属性是只有某些选择才有的。

比如：乘车时间是所有可能的通勤方式中都有的一个属性，然而等待时间是只有乘坐公共交通才有的属性。

4). Decision rule

An individual invokes a decision rule to select an alternative. The decision rule may include random choice, variety seeking, or other processes which we refer to as being irrational. A number of possible rules, such as utility maximization, fallC:\Users\dhlsf\AppData\ under the "rational decision" processes. Utility maximization rule is based on two fundamental concepts:

  • The attribute vector characterizing each alternative can be reduced to a scaler utility value for that alternative. Presuming that individuals make "trade-offs" among the attributes.
  • An individual selects that alternative with highest utility value.

决策者通常都有一个规则去进行选择，有些规则是非理性的：如随机选择、寻求多样化等非理性的决策规则，但是也有很多规则是理性的，如：效用最大化：

  • 描述每个选择的属性（向量）要能够变为一个效用值（标量），假设所有的决策者都是在每个属性间做权衡取舍。

  • 每个决策者都最大化自己的效用值

     

2. Utility-Based Choice Theory

Utility is an indicator of value to an individual. The utility maximization rule states that an individual will select the alternative from his/her set of available alternatives that maximizes his or her utility.

The utility function contains attributes of alternatives and characteristics of individuals that describes individual's utility evaluation for each alternatives.

效用最大化，即满意程度最大化。对于某种出行方式而言，费用越低、出行时间越短、安全性越好、可靠性越高，该方式的的效用就越高。效用可以表示为不同决策者特征、不同选项不同属性的函数。

2.2.1 Why is included?

Deterministic portion of utility function alone is insufficient to model complex real world decision scenarios:

  • The individual may have incomplete or incorrect information or misperceptions about the attributes of some or all of the alternatives.
  • The analyst is unlikely to know, or account for, specific circumstance of the individual's travel decision. For example, someone may made irrational choice.

Thus, it's necessary to include a stochastic term

2.3 Multinomial Logit Model

2.3.1 Model description

If we assume the error term is normally distributed, this leads to the formulation of the Multinomial Probit (MNP) choice model. If we assume the error term has a Gumbel (or Extreme-Value) distribution, this leads to the formulation of Multinomial Logit Model(MNL)

误差项不同，所对应的model也就不同。如果误差项服从的是正态分布，那么对应的就是MNP选择模型；如果误差项服从Gumbel分布，那么对应的就是MNL选择模型。

The specific assumptions that lead to the Multinomial Logit Model (MNL) are:

MNL模型包括以下假设：

  • The error components follow a Gumbel distribution

    误差项服从 Gumbel 分布

  • The error components are independently distributed across individuals and alternatives .

    不同决策者（选项）的误差项独立分布

  • The error components are identically distributed across across individuals and alternatives .

    不同决策者（选项）的误差项服从相同分布（参数也一样）

The MNL gives the choice probability of each alternative as a SoftMax function of the deterministic portion of the utility of all the alternatives

2.3.3 The Equivalent Difference Property

A fundamental property of the multinomial logit and other choice model is that the choice probabilities of the alternatives depend only on the differences in the deterministic utilities of different alternatives and not their actual values.

选择模型的基本特性是：选择每种选项的概率仅取决于不同选项间效用的差异，而不取决于其绝对值

 

2.4 Parameter estimate--Maximum Likelihood Estimation Theory

In the previous examples we've seen the utility function was given with the already estimated parameters and what we did was using those estimated utility functions to calculate the probabilities and evaluate different policies. How can we actually estimate a multinomial logit model?

在前面的例子中，我们见到的效用函数参数值已经给定，我们做的是使用估计好的效用函数来计算每种选项被选中的概率并用它们评估各种经营策略。实际应该如何估计 MNL 模型的参数呢？

Finding model parameters which maximize the likelihood of the observed choices conditioned on the model.

给定一个模型，找到一组参数使得观察到的选项出现的可能性最大

  • Develop a joint probability density function of the observed sample, called the likelihood function.

    建立一个所有样本被同时观察的联合概率密度函数，叫做似然函数

  • Estimate parameter values which maximize the likelihood function.

    找到使似然函数最大的参数，即我们待估的参数

 

The likelihood function is,

似然函数为：

Where

is chosen indicator (=1 if is chosen by individual and 0, otherwise) 意味着决策者 选了 这个选项

is the probability that individual chooses alternative 决策者 选择选项 的概率

 

Estimating parameters:

估计参数：

The values of the parameters which maximize the likelihood function are obtained by finding the first derivative of the likelihood function and equating it to zero.

通过求似然函数的梯度，令梯度等于0，得到参数的最优值

Log function is strictly monotonic, the maximizer of log function also maximizers the original function. Taking log of likelihood function transforms production into summation. The derivative of summation is easier to compute.

对数函数是单调函数，所以使对数函数最大的点也会让原函数最大。对似然函数取对数后，累乘变成了累加，更容易求导。

    tB.grad = None

    with t.no_grad():
        tB -= learning_rate * tB.grad
        tB.grad = None
