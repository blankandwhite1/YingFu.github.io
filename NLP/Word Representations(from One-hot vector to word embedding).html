This representation will have several disadvantages:

(1) Each word is completely independent. This word representation does not give any notion of similarity.

(2) Vector will become large and sparse while the vocabulary becomes large. So there does a need to find a subspace that encodes the relationship and reduce the dimensionality of Vector.

2. Word embedding

Word embeddings are in fact a class of techniques where individual words are represented as real-valued vectors in a predefined vector space where words having the same meaning have a similar representation. For example, "king" is similar with "queen" and "man" is similar with "woman".

Methods to generate this mapping include word co-occurrence matrix decomposition and neural networks and etc.

2.4 Association with Auto-encoder

As shown before, the ultimate goal of skip-gram or CBOW model is not to predict context words or center word , but to learn intelligent vector representation of words. Up to now, another model which has the similarities in the dense representation and type of network appeared in my head, that is the . The structure of auto-encoder is shown below:

So next I will discuss their similarities and differences.

Similarities:

What actually auto-encoder do is to represent input dataset in a compressed form retaining the most information, which is called encoder. Then we use the latent representation to reconstruct the input, which is called decoder. The purpose of training an auto-encoder whose output value is equal to input value is to learn the latent representation of the input. One way for auto-encoders to obtain useful features is to let dimension of less than the input .

Similarly, Word2Vec converts a sparse unique indexing of the vocabulary to a continuous, dense and distributed representation that can be considered to be a compressed latent representation of words in the vocabulary.

Differences:

(1) The purposes differs. The purpose of auto-encoder is to reconstruct the input to learn the latent representation, while the word2vec is to predict context word given center word or predict center word given context word

(2) The Input data types differs: Auto-encoder can be applied to any sort of dataset to learn a latent representation, while word2vec is used only for words.

(3) The loss functions differs. In auto-encoder, the loss function is the reconstruction error, while in word2vec, the loss function is the cross entropy loss of the soft-max classifier.
